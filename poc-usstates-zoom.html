US with Zoom

This map of the US is constructed in the same manner as the previous map, but adds a "click-to-zoom" feature. The race data is from the USA Today US Census API - 2010.

  • Republican states are in red, Democratic states are in blue, and states that are both Republican and Democrat are in purple.
  • Hover over a state to get quick info.
  • Click on a state to zoom in.
  • Click in the white area to zoom back out.
